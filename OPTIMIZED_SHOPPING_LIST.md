## 💡 SHOPPING TIPS

1. **Fresh vegetables first**: Use tomatoes, spinach, and mushrooms within 3-5 days
2. **Rotation strategy**: Buy fresh items for immediate meals, use preserved items for later in the week
3. **Meal prep opportunities**:
   - Lentil soup (red lentils cook in 20 min)
   - Vegetable stir-fries (peppers, zucchini, carrots)
   - Hummus with fresh vegetables
   - Couscous with mixed vegetables
   - Pasta with tomato sauce
   - Rice and beans
   - Egg omelets with vegetables and cheese

4. **Storage tips**:
   - Keep vegetables in separate crisper drawers
   - Store bread in freezer if you won't use it within 3 days
   - Keep dairy products cold (35-40°F)
   - Store grains in cool, dry place
